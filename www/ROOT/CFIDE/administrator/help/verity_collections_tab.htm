Verity Collections tab

To specify which Verity collections are included in the archive definition:

  1. In the Verity Collections tab, select one or more of the Verity collections to include  in your archive definition.

  1. To proceed, do one of the following:

  1. Click other tabs on the Archive Wizard to continue defining the archive definition.

  2. Click Close Window to save the information and exit the Archive Wizard. The name of the archive definition appears in the Current Archive Definition List.
