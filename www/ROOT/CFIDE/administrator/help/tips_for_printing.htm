Tips for printing

You can print ColdFusion Administrator online Help topics and information from the web browser. The print options are determined by the browser version on your system.

Print a topic in the right pane, do one of the following

  • Right-click the topic and click Print.

  • Click the topic and select File > Print.

  • Click the topic and click the Print icon.

Print a topic from the Contents tab

  • Right-click the topic and click Print Target.

 

For more information about printing, see the printing topic in the online Help system that is provided with your browser.
