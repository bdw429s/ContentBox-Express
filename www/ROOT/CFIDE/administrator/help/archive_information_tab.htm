Archive Information tab

Enter information in the Archive Wizard Information tab

  1. In the Archive Information tab, do the following:

  • In the Description list box (optional), provide explanatory information about this archive definition, such as date and time, creator, reason for archive, and so on.

  • Click Select All to automatically enable all of the options that are located on each of the Archive Wizard tabs.

  • Click Deselect All to automatically disable all of the options that are located on each of the Archive Wizard tabs.

  1. To proceed, do one of the following:

  • Click other tabs in the Archive Wizard navigation pane to continue defining the archive definition.

  • Click Close Window to save the archive information and exit the Archive Wizard. The name of the archive appears in the Current Archive Definition List.
