Archive Summary  tab

The Archive Summary tab lets you to view options in the archive definition. After reviewing the settings on this tab, you can:

  • Change or add to the settings in the definition by returning to the appropriate tab on the Archive Definition page and making the adjustment.

  • Click other tabs on the Archive Wizard to continue defining the archive definition.

  • Click Close Window to save the information and exit the Archive wizard. The name of the archive definition appears in the Current Archive Definition List.

 

Tip: After you save an archive definition, you can archive the data specified in the definition by returning to the Archives and Deployment page and clicking Build Archive.
