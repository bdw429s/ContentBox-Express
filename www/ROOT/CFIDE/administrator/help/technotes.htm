TechNotes

The TechNotes area (formerly, Knowledge Base) provides valuable information that you can use to solve problems and answer questions on a variety of topics. You can search for information by entering keywords or an article number.

To access the TechNotes area:

  • Click the TechNotes link in the top pane of the ColdFusion Administrator.
