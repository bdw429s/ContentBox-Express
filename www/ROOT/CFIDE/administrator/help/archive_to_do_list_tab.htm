Archive To Do List tab

The Archive To Do List page lets you make of list of things you want done before or after finalizing an archive definition or a restore operation.

Use the Archive To Do List page

  1. In the Archive To Do List page, specify the information you want to track about this archive in the Pre - restore list box or the Post - restore list box.

  2. To proceed, do one of the following:

  • Click other tabs on the Archive Wizard to continue defining the archive definition.

  • Click Close Window to save the information and exit the Archive Wizard. The name of the archive definition appears in the Current Archive Definition List.

Tips:

  • You can use this page to remind the person restoring the archive of the things that must be done before or after restoring a site. For example, if the archive included variables, you could remind the person to define the variables on the restore machine prior to restoring the archive.

  • You can use this page to list items that you have to verify or include in an archive definition prior to finalizing the archive definition.
